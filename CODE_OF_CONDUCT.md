# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, provide constructive technical feedback, and avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and disclosure of private information.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behaviour may be reported privately to the repository owner. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled proportionately. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This project follows the principles of the Contributor Covenant.
